## 📊 Features Explained

### Customer Segmentation
The system organizes your customers into groups based on their buying habits. You can identify who your top clients are and which customers may need more attention. This helps you focus your efforts where they matter most.

### KPI Tracking
Key Performance Indicators help you track the health of your sales process. The dashboard displays charts for total revenue, conversion rates, and average deal sizes. These numbers update automatically as you add new information.

### AI Insights
The software analyzes your historical data to predict future trends. It points out patterns you might miss. These insights help you make decisions backed by data instead of guesses.

### Email Automation
The tool connects to your SendGrid account to send messages. You can set up triggers so customers receive emails after specific events. This removes the manual task of writing the same message to many people.

## 📁 Preparing Your Data

The dashboard works best with clean information. Format your sales data in a spreadsheet file such as CSV or Excel. Include columns for customer names, dates of purchase, and dollar amounts. 

1. Save your file in a known location on your computer.
2. Click the Upload Data button inside the dashboard.
3. Select your file from the pop-up window.
4. The dashboard will process your information and generate updated charts.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### Does the system store my data on the internet?
The application runs locally on your computer. Your sales information stays on your hard drive and does not upload to a third-party server, except for the emails you choose to send through SendGrid.

### What happens if the dashboard stops responding?
If the charts stop updating, refresh your browser page. If the problem continues, close the black command window on your taskbar and restart the application using the desktop shortcut.

### How do I update the software?
Check the main download page periodically for new versions. Download the new installer and run it over your current version to update the features.

### Can I connect to other services?
The dashboard currently supports manual file uploads. You can export data from your primary CRM system and load it into this dashboard to receive the benefits of the analysis tools.

## 🛠️ Troubleshooting

If you encounter issues, look here for solutions.

- Installation fails: Ensure you have administrator rights on your Windows account. Contact your IT support if you cannot run installation files.
- Missing charts: Ensure your spreadsheet includes the required column headers. Re-export your data from your CRM if the upload returns an error.
- Email errors: Verify your SendGrid API key is correct. You can enter or update this key in the Settings tab of the dashboard.
- Slow performance: Avoid keeping too many tabs open in your web browser while using the application. Close background programs that use high memory.
